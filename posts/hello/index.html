Yuki`s first story

2023-03-02 :: Author:Yuki :: 1 min read (151 words)

Yuki was a curious and creative 7-year-old girl who loved to make things with her hands. One day, she decided to make a boat using clay, and she went to work.

  • Step 1: Yuki took a ball of clay and rolled it into a long, thin shape like a snake.

  • Step 2: Then, she bent the clay into a U-shape to make the hull of the boat, and used a small tool to create a seat for a tiny clay figurine to sit.

  • Step 3: Finally, Yuki added a small piece of clay on top of the hull to make the mast of the boat, and she gently pressed a small triangle shape of clay to create a sail.

Yuki was thrilled with her new boat, and she placed the tiny clay figurine in the seat. She imagined the figurine sailing the high seas and discovering new lands. Yuki was proud
